· 02 — mechanism

Three steps.
No dashboards to babysit.

01

Watch

A curated roster of top-performing wallets is tracked continuously. New buys and sells are detected within seconds of execution.

> latency < 60s
02

Filter

Proprietary guards reject the noise — junk prices, tiny positions, both-sides bets, overexposure, cold-streak wallets. Only ~2% of signals survive.

> signal_pass_rate = 2.1%
03

Mirror

Survivors become real orders on your own account. Hot wallets get sized up, cold ones cooled. Winnings auto-claim on resolution. You sleep.

> execution = automatic
